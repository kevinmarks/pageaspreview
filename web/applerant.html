How Apple's iOS fragmentation problems distort design thinking

As someone who uses both Android and iOS regularly, I'm getting increasingly frustrated by fragmentation. Oddly though, contrary to popular myth,it's not on my Android devices I see this, but on the iOS ones. I install a popular, well-funded application like Instagram or Circa on my iPad, but when I launch it 3/4 of the screen is black bars, with a teensy little app in the middle.
